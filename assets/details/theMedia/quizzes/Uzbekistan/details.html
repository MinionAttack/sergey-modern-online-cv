Online quiz "Do you know Uzbekistan?"

Information

  • Country: The republic of Uzbekistan
  • Department: The Cabinet Of Ministers
  • Organiser: Committee On International Relations And Friendship Relations With Foreign Countries
  • Year: 2021

During my last position as a research support technician, a PhD student from Uzbekistan who was working in the group entered me and other colleagues in a online quiz competition. I came second and, after a waiting time due to COVID-19 restrictions, we received the gifts and sent a photo as proof which was published in the news announcing the prize-giving.

You can read the news of the award ceremony, translated into English by Google Translate, on the official website .
